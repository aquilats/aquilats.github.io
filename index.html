Fleet Manager

Aquila Fleet Manager is a cloud-based tool designed to help run your trucking business by managing field tickets, billing, employee payroll, fleet maintenance, and more. Aquila Fleet Manager allows you to upload attachments, securing your paperwork and giving you access to the data you need right when you need it. Reporting tools including job location maps provide priceless statistics that help you reduce costs and increase your business.

Motor Manager

Aquila Motor Manager is a cloud-based asset tracking tool and work-flow manager designed specifically for drilling motors. It was developed in collaboration with industry experts with decades of experience to capture the most critical aspects of managing a motor business. This solution allows you to manage your inventory, track jobs, and store, analyze and visualize vital data at each step of the process. Aquila's Motor Manager lets you focus on growing your business.
